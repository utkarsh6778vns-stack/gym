Who We Are

A gym for people who actually lift.

Iron House started in a 2,000 sq ft warehouse with second-hand equipment and a dream. Seven years later, we've expanded to 12,000 sq ft but kept the same energy.

No juice bar. No towel service. No crowds around the cable machines because everyone's too scared to use the squat racks.

What we do have: chalk, deadlift platforms, competition benches, and members who rerack their weights.
